How Degree Apprenticeships work

1. Apply or be nominated

You can apply directly or be sponsored by your employer. We support both new apprentices and staff development routes.

2. Study alongside work

Study is delivered through blended learning — digital teaching, on-campus workshops and workplace assessment.

3. Earn and achieve

Apprentices remain employed and paid while completing degree-level modules and professional assessments.

4. Career progression

Graduates progress into higher-skilled roles, management or professional registration depending on the programme.
